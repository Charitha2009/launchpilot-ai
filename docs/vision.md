# LaunchPilot AI

## Vision

LaunchPilot AI is an AI-powered landing page auditing platform.

Before marketers publish a landing page, LaunchPilot analyzes the page and identifies issues affecting performance, accessibility, SEO, tracking, and user experience.

The goal is to eliminate manual quality assurance by automatically detecting problems and providing actionable recommendations.

The application is designed to assist Forward Deployed Engineers, marketers, and growth teams by reducing launch risk and speeding up confident deployments.
