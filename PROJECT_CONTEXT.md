# LaunchPilot AI

## Vision

LaunchPilot AI is an AI-powered landing page quality assurance platform.

The application helps marketers and Forward Deployed Engineers validate landing pages before deployment.

Instead of manually checking analytics, SEO, responsiveness, accessibility, and tracking, LaunchPilot automatically audits the page and provides actionable recommendations.

---

## Target Users

- Marketing Teams
- Growth Teams
- Forward Deployed Engineers
- Agencies

---

## MVP Workflow

User enters a landing page URL.

↓

Application validates URL.

↓

Playwright opens page.

↓

Screenshot captured.

↓

Lighthouse audit runs.

↓

Tracking scripts detected.

↓

Gemini reviews screenshot.

↓

Results displayed.

↓

PDF generated.
